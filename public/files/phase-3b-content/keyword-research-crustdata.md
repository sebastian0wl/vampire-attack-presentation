## Competitive Intelligence Notes

**CrustData's own SEO play:** They're publishing comparison blog posts aggressively — "Clay alternatives", "People Data Labs alternatives", "Scrapin alternatives", "B2B data API providers ranked." They're trying to own the comparison keyword space for B2B data.

**Gap they can't fill:** NONE of their content addresses consumer data, B2B2C identity resolution, or non-B2B use cases. This is our uncontested lane.

**Versium alert:** Versium (versium.com) also claims B2B2C identity graph with 2B+ contact points and now has an MCP server. They're the closest competitor to Minerva's positioning on the consumer data + B2B2C angle. Monitor their content closely.

**Pricing intelligence:** CrustData starts at $95-200/mo with credit-based pricing. Their transparency here is a strength — Minerva should consider publishing pricing or at least a pricing framework to compete on this dimension.
