## What Minerva has that nobody else does

### 1. B2B2C Identity Graph
- Connects personal identifiers (personal email, home address, phone, mobile ad ID, IP) with professional identifiers (work email, employer, job title, LinkedIn) on the SAME person
- Nobody else does this. Apollo/ZoomInfo = B2B only. Windfall/Acxiom = B2C only. Crustdata = B2B only.
- This is WHY our match rate is 70-80% vs 20-30%. We can match on whatever identifier the customer gives us.

### 3. The Demand Generation Platform
Three workflows that no enrichment-only provider offers:
- **Paid media optimization:** Rebuild Meta/Google audiences from real consumer data, not black-box targeting
- **Direct mail targeting:** Score and segment households by income, wealth, and buying propensity
- **Lead triage:** Score inbound leads by ICP fit and route to the right sales rep by product tier

### 4. Agentic Data Engineer
- Automates CDP implementation (connecting CRM, marketing automation, ad platforms, Stripe, etc.)
- Cuts implementation from months to days
- Uses agents to reason over table schemas, identify join keys, and unify first-party data
- Then enriches the unified record with Minerva's third-party data

### 5. Analytics Agent
- Natural language interface to ask questions about your unified data
- "How is my wealthy suburban demographic responding to Meta campaigns?"
- "Which product tier should I route this lead to?"
- Replaces the analyst work of pulling reports across platforms
