## 3 Key Differentiators

**1. The Other 80% of the Profile**
CrustData tells your agent about the company and the job title. Minerva adds income, property value, household composition, interests, purchase behavior, and 2,168 total fields. If your agent qualifies leads, personalizes offers, or scores buying power, the consumer layer is where the signal lives.

**2. Best-in-Class Contact Graph**
733M emails, 899M phones, 270M+ addresses. Personal and professional, all linked. Most teams run a waterfall across 2-3 contact providers or just live with gaps. Minerva replaces the waterfall. One call, one vendor, most complete contact record in the market. CrustData requires LinkedIn URLs or work emails. Personal email? Returns nothing. 70-80% match rate on mixed files vs 20-30% from B2B-only providers.

**3. Proven at Scale**
$20M raised. Named customers across sports (Hard Rock Stadium/NBA), travel (Wander), recruiting (Juicebox), and real estate (Luxury Presence). Partnerships with Experian, NBA, and Clay. CrustData is pre-revenue transparency: company names appear in product demos but no confirmed customer endorsements or published case studies, and no third-party reviews.

## When to Choose CrustData vs When to Choose Minerva

### Choose CrustData if:
- Your use case is purely B2B firmographic enrichment (company data, headcount, funding, tech stack)
- You need real-time company signals (job changes, funding rounds, hiring surges)
- All your input identifiers are LinkedIn URLs or work emails
- You need a Web Search API for agent consumption
- You're optimizing for lowest cost on B2B-only lookups

### Choose Minerva if:
- Your agent interacts with prospects and leads as people, not just job titles
- You need income, wealth, property, interests, or behavioral data on a person
- Your input files include personal emails, phone numbers, or home addresses
- You need 70-80% match rates instead of 20-30% on mixed-identifier files -- run the head-to-head test on your actual list; we're confident we're better
- You need a provider with named customers and enterprise partnerships you can reference
- You care about CCPA compliance documentation
